Disney Guessing Game

Welcome Message:

I created a game using a fanmade Disney API. This game will test your knowledge of every single Disney character ever created from 1923 to 2023.

How to play:

Start by hitting let's play at the top. You will first with 3 lives and be presented with a picture and 4 options. Guess correctly to score points. Use these points to score buy power-ups from the shop to get an advantage in game.
